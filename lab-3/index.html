Fitness Tips

1. Stay hydrated throughout the day, especially during workouts.
2. Incorporate strength training into your routine to build muscle and increase metabolism.
3. Prioritize sleep for muscle recovery and overall well-being.
4. Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.
5. Set realistic goals and track your progress to stay motivated.
